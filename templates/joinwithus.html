EVERGREEN

1. Commitment to the Mission:

- I will uphold and support the mission and values of [NGO Name].
- I will actively contribute to the achievement of our organization's goals.

2. Respect and Inclusivity:

- I will treat all members, volunteers, and beneficiaries with respect and dignity.
- I will promote diversity, equity, and inclusivity within our organization.

3. Ethical Behavior:

- I will act with honesty, integrity, and transparency in all my interactions.
- I will follow all applicable laws and regulations.

4. Collaboration and Teamwork:

- I will work collaboratively with fellow members and volunteers.
- I will contribute positively to a respectful and supportive team environment.

5. Confidentiality:

- I will respect the confidentiality of sensitive information related to our organization and its beneficiaries.

6. Fundraising and Donor Relations:

- I will engage in ethical fundraising practices, ensuring that donor contributions are used for their intended purposes.
- I will respect donor privacy and preferences.

7. Compliance:

- I will adhere to the policies and guidelines set forth by [NGO Name].
- I will promptly report any concerns or violations of our code of conduct.

8. Professionalism:

- I will represent [NGO Name] in a professional and positive manner.
- I will continuously seek opportunities for personal and professional development.

9. Termination of Membership:

- reserves the right to terminate the membership of individuals who violate this code of conduct.
